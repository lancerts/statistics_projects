B-IPS: The Matlab function of B-IPS for log-affine model fitting. 
A demonstration is provided in Demo_general_BIPS.m.

PoissonL1: The Matlab function of L1-IPS. 
A demonstration is provided by Demo_L1-IPS.m.

The subfolder minFunc_2012 contains routines of Newton/L-BFGS (see Schmidt (2012) in the references).
